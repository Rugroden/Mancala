moving:
	-take only from your side.
	-move counter clockwise
	-place seeds in pits along
	the path except for the 
	enemy's store.
	-if you end in your store 
	you get another turn.
	-if you end in an empty pit
	on your side you get the 
	seeds opposite you and the 
	single seed. place in store.
	-game ends when a side is 
	empty
	-opponent takes the seeds on
	their side into their store
	-who ever has the most seeds
	is the winner.
